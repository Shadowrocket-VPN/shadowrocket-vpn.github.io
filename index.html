Difference Between VPN & Proxy Server

Many users confuse VPNs and proxy servers, but they function differently. A VPN (Virtual Private Network) encrypts all internet traffic from your device and routes it through a secure server. This provides complete device-level protection.

A proxy server, on the other hand, acts as an intermediary between your device and the internet. It hides your IP address but may not encrypt traffic unless configured with secure protocols.

VPNs are generally more secure because they encrypt all data traffic, whereas proxies are faster but offer limited security. Shadowrocket is unique because it combines both VPN and proxy functionalities, giving users advanced flexibility.
